Martin Forsell

A Indie/folk singer-songwriter from Sweden.

Martin Forsell is a singer-songwriter who's been in different constellations over the years, but since early 2015 working on his solo project, which has a more sense of purity and honesty in both lyrics and music than anything he's created before.

It has a folksy pop retro sound inspired by bands such as Nathaniel Rateliff & The Night Sweats, Edward Sharpe & The Magnetic Zeros and The Tallest Man on Earth.
